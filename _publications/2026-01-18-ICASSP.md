The work "Diffusion Stochastic Learning over Multi-Team Network Games" is accepted to [ICASSP 2026](https://2026.ieeeicassp.org/technical-program/), with paper number 10037. One can find the details to the poster session in the link above: the poster session is SPTM-P2: Distributed Optimization I, at Tue., May 5th, 14:00 to 16:00 at Poster Area 2.

The author Wen Perng completed this work as a visiting student from National Taiwan University at the Adaptive Systems Laboratory at EPFL, Switzerland.

The [MATLAB code](https://github.com/WenPerng/ASL_2025/tree/main/Diffusion_Stochastic_Learning_over_Multi-Team_Network_Games) for the figures used in the paper can be found here, the proofs to the lemmas and theorems can be downloaded [here](/files/pdfs/papers/2026_ICASSP_proof.pdf) (last updated: 2026/04/19).
